About dustpan

dustpan is an OS X application aimed at cleaning up your cluttered desktop. While running, the application will periodically dust your desktop, moving all files that haven't been used in a while to the dustpan, where they can be freely accessed or returned to the desktop as needed.

dustpan ensures a clean desktop without deleting anything, touching your desktop folders, or hiding your important, frequently-accessed files.
